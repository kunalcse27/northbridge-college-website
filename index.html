Northbridge College, based in Mumbai, is a progressive institution committed to delivering high-quality education in science, technology, and innovation. The college operates through several specialized schools such as Innovative Studies, Continuing Education, Mass Education, and Professional Studies, fostering entrepreneurship and cutting-edge knowledge.

Northbridge emphasizes a blend of theoretical learning and hands-on experience, preparing students for careers in India and globally. With strategic affiliations and collaborations with reputed universities and industry partners, the college provides students access to advanced resources and professional networks.

Our Mission

At Northbridge College, our mission is to empower the next generation of engineers with innovative thinking, strong ethical values, and real-world problem-solving skills. We strive to provide a transformative education through cutting-edge technology, industry-focused curriculum, and a nurturing academic environment. Our goal is to foster leadership, creativity, and lifelong learning to shape professionals who will contribute meaningfully to society and the global engineering landscape

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in engineering education and innovation, recognized globally for academic excellence, cutting-edge research, and community impact. At Northbridge College of Engineering, we envision creating a dynamic learning ecosystem that inspires students to become visionary engineers, ethical leaders, and responsible global citizens who drive sustainable progress and technological advancement. To be a global hub for engineering excellence, driving transformative innovation
